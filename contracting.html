Three routes: compete us directly on solicitations (we actively bid DLA DIBBS and monitor SAM.gov opportunities under our NAICS codes), contact our Government Business POC with a requirement for quote, or engage us as a subcontractor/teaming partner on your prime effort. Our UEI (MRNLCHP6N8V5) and CAGE (9NWB7) are active in SAM.gov — verification takes minutes.
