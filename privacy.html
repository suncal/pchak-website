Privacy Policy.

What we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. Short version: we collect only what you send us, and we don't sell it to anyone.

EffectiveJuly 2026
Applies Topchakconsulting.com

01Who We Are

P CHAK CONSULTING LLC ("we," "us") is a Georgia, USA limited liability company. This policy describes how we handle information in connection with this website and inquiries you send us.

02Information We Collect

  • Information you provide. When you contact us by form, email, or phone, we receive the details you choose to share — such as your name, organization, email address, phone number, and the content of your message.
  • Technical information. Our hosting provider may log standard technical data (IP address, browser type, pages requested) for security and operational purposes. This site does not set tracking cookies and does not run analytics or advertising scripts.

03How We Use Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and conduct business you've requested;
  • To prepare proposals, quotes, and contract documentation;
  • To meet legal, contractual, and federal-contracting obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

04Sharing

We share information only with service providers who help us operate (such as website hosting and email), when required by law or regulation, or as needed to perform a contract you are part of. Third-party services used by this site include our web host and Google Fonts (which serves font files and may log technical request data).

05Retention & Security

We keep correspondence as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, and we apply reasonable safeguards appropriate to a firm of our size. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

06Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you've sent us by contacting info@pchakconsulting.com. We'll honor requests consistent with our legal and contractual obligations.

07Changes & Contact

We may update this policy from time to time; the effective date above reflects the latest revision. Questions? Write to info@pchakconsulting.com or call (800) 988-2863.
